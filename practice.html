Human-Sized Skeleton Companion

A custom-built, Raspberry Pi–powered display piece that’s evolving into a full humanoid robot.

Meet your room’s most intriguing companion: a life-sized, anatomically correct skeleton with brains you can talk to. At its core sits a Raspberry Pi connected to a microphone and speaker, integrating with the ChatGPT API for natural, conversational interaction. It doesn’t move—yet—but the roadmap is clear: progressively add actuation, sensing, and behaviors on the way to a full-fledged humanoid robot.

Each build is made to order and hand-assembled. You can customize the outer skeleton’s finish and accents to match your style—clean lab-white, aged patina, neon cyberpunk, or something uniquely yours. Thanks to ChatGPT, your companion learns your preferences over time and becomes more helpful, more entertaining, and more “you.”

Details and Roadmap

Today: a striking, intelligent showpiece that can chat, play audio, answer questions, and host custom prompts or routines. Tomorrow: motorized joints, onboard sensors, posture and gesture control, and a safety-first control stack. The design is intentionally modular—add servos, drivers, and feedback loops as your build evolves.

For developers and tinkerers, it’s a friendly platform: GPIO for sensors and lights, USB for peripherals, and a straightforward software stack connecting to the ChatGPT API. Fork the repo, contribute modules, and shape the path to a fully embodied assistant.

Ordering and Customization

Each unit is built to order. Lead times vary depending on component availability and customization complexity. We’ll finalize the finish, lighting accents, and internal wiring style with you before assembly.
