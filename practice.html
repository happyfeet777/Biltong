Human-Sized Humanoid Companion

We build custom, Raspberry Pi–powered companions on the path to a full humanoid robot.

Meet your room’s most intriguing companion: a life-sized skeleton with brains you can talk to. At its core sits a Raspberry Pi connected to a microphone and speaker, integrating with the ChatGPT API for natural, conversational interaction. It doesn’t move—yet—but the roadmap is clear: progressively add actuation, sensing, and behaviors on the way to a full-fledged humanoid robot.

Built to order and hand-assembled, the bones remain authentic — the personality is all you. Customization happens on the outside: dress it in normal clothes, choose face styles (male, female, or neutral), and add accessories like a Viking helmet, masks, or lighting accents. With ChatGPT or local LLM support, your companion gets to know you over time, becoming more helpful, more entertaining, and more “you.”

Core Compute

Raspberry Pi at the heart, with configurable storage and network. Easy to update and extend.

Voice I/O

Integrated mic and speaker for hands-free conversation and responses.

AI Integration

ChatGPT API for natural dialogue. Learns your preferences over time.

Exterior Style

Clothing, faceplates, helmets, masks, and lighting — your call.

Details and Roadmap

Today: a striking, intelligent showpiece that can chat, play audio, answer questions, and host custom prompts or routines. Tomorrow: motorized joints, onboard sensors, posture and gesture control, and a safety-first control stack. The design is intentionally modular—add servos, drivers, and feedback loops as your build evolves.

For developers and tinkerers, it’s a friendly platform: GPIO for sensors and lights, USB for peripherals, and a straightforward software stack connecting to the ChatGPT API. Fork the repo, contribute modules, and shape the path to a fully embodied assistant.

Ordering and Customization

Each unit is built to order. Lead times vary depending on component availability and customization complexity. We’ll finalize clothing, face style, and accessories (helmets, masks, lighting) with you before assembly.
